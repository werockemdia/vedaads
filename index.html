Everything You Need To Know Before Working With Us

Quick answers to common questions.

1 What is performance marketing for e‑commerce?

It’s spending on ads where you can see exactly what you get back—more sales, leads or sign-ups. You pay for results, not just visibility.

2 How is it different from traditional marketing?

Traditional marketing is hard to measure. Performance marketing is built around tracking: we know which ads bring sales and we keep improving them.

3 How do you measure success?

We look at sales generated, cost per sale and return on ad spend—so you know you’re making profit, not just getting clicks.

4 Why work with Veda Ads?

We act as your growth partner. We care about your profitability and will tell you if something’s off. We want your business healthy and growing.

5 What industries do you work with?

We specialise in D2C brands across fashion, jewellery, beauty & wellness, food & beverages, home decor, pet, SaaS & apps and more. If your product sells online and you care about profit, we can usually help.

6 What’s your minimum ad spend and fees?

Our plans start from ₹34,000/month, with performance fees kicking in only after a minimum ad spend threshold per plan. This keeps us aligned on profitable scale instead of just higher budgets.

7 How long before we see results?

Most brands start seeing clear signal in the first 4–6 weeks as we test offers, audiences and creatives. Significant scale typically happens over 3–6 months as we double down on winners and cut what doesn’t work.

8 Can you work with our existing in‑house team or agency?

Yes. Many clients bring us in to own one part of the funnel—performance ads, creatives, landing pages or retention—while existing teams handle the rest. We’re used to collaborating with founders, in‑house marketers and other partners.

9 What tools and platforms do you use?

We work across Meta, Google, Amazon and key analytics/attribution tools depending on your stack. We plug into your current setup where it makes sense, and suggest improvements only when they clearly improve tracking or ROI.
